How PupChat Began

PupChat was founded in 2022 by a group of dog enthusiasts who noticed a gap in the social media landscape. While there were platforms for general social networking, none focused specifically on the needs and interests of dog owners and lovers.

What started as a small community of local dog owners sharing photos and meetup information has grown into a vibrant global network with thousands of active users.

Our mission is simple: to create a safe, friendly space where dog lovers can connect, share, and learn from each other.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike other social networks, PupChat is designed specifically for dog lovers. Every feature is tailored to enhance the experience of sharing your dog's life and connecting with other canine enthusiasts.

From breed-specific groups to local dog park meetups, health and training tips to photo contests, we've built a platform that celebrates everything about our furry friends.

Our strict content moderation ensures a positive environment free from the negativity often found on other platforms.

Our Mission

To create the world's most engaged community of dog lovers, where every member feels welcome to share their passion, knowledge, and love for dogs.
