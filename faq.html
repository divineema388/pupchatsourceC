Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PupChat

How do I create a PupChat account?

To create a PupChat account, click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage or in the mobile app. You'll need to provide a valid email address, create a username and password, and add some basic information about you and your dog(s). After verifying your email, your account will be ready to use!

Is PupChat free to use?

Yes! PupChat is completely free to use. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free for our community of dog lovers.

How do I join or create a group?

To join a group, go to the "Groups" section and browse available groups. You can search by breed, location, or interest. To create your own group, click the "Create Group" button, choose a name and description, and set your group preferences (public/private).

Can I use PupChat without sharing my dog's information?

While we encourage sharing information about your dog to get the most out of PupChat, you can choose which details to include. The only required information is your email address and a username for your account.

How do I report inappropriate content or users?

You can report content by clicking the three dots (...) on any post and selecting "Report." To report a user, go to their profile and click the "Report" button. Our moderation team reviews all reports promptly.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

How can I connect with other dog owners in my area?

PupChat has location-based features to help you connect with nearby dog owners. Enable location services in your account settings, then browse the "Nearby" section to see local users, groups, and dog park meetups.

Can I use PupChat on multiple devices?

Yes! Your PupChat account syncs across all your devices. Just download the app or visit the website on any device and log in with your account credentials.
